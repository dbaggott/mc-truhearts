Two small fixes based on player feedback.

- The "TruHearts: on/off" toast now shows in **every gamemode**, not just survival. Previously the toast was silent in creative and spectator — the keybind fired and the state flipped, but you had no on-screen confirmation until you switched back to survival and noticed the HP overlay was gone. The toast now attaches to the same slot as vanilla's held-item-name message so it renders wherever that does.
- Longer, more legible toast: **1.5 s total** (1 s at full opacity + 0.5 s fade), up from 0.75 s.

The HP overlay itself is unchanged — it still shows only when vanilla hearts are visible (i.e., not in creative or spectator).
